Opinion essay about technology

Opinion essay about technology este exemplul nostru pentru eseul de care ai nevoie la limba engleză. Dacă vrei să afli cum am structurat noi eseul despre tehnologie citește cu încredere acest material. În cadrul eseului vor fi abordate teme precum: internetul, telefoanele inteligente, inovațiile din medicină, dar și dezavantajele pe care le implică utilizarea excesivă a tehnologiei.

Opinion essay about technology

The technological revolution has impacted society in a way that few other things have. From smartphones to the internet and everything in between, we use technology daily to make our lives easier, keep in touch with friends and family, have access to different forms of entertainment, and so on.

However, technology has also brought upon us severe privacy concerns, has made it easier for hackers and other malicious people to do bad things and has made a lot of young people addicted to their gadgets to the extent that they could not live without them.

I will try to explore both sides of the argument, since they are equally relevant:

First of all, look at the internet today and how far it has come since its early days. You can do almost anything imaginable on it. 

I am truly thankful for the existence of the internet, as it gives me the possibility to stay in touch with friends and relatives living in other countries basically for free. The only thing you have to pay is the subscription, otherwise communication is free.

I also shop a lot online because I think it is more convenient. I cannot always find what I am looking for in the stores from my city, and having the possibility to search for unique t-shirts online is truly a blessing if you care about how you dress.

But the internet would not mean anything unless you had a device to connect to it. As such, I will move on to another piece of tech that I find amazing: smartphones.

It is amazing how something that can fit in your pocket can do so many things. You can use it to find your way home if you are lost through its GPS functionality. You can make calls, send text messages, write emails and other materials and even do business related stuff, such as presentations. I repeat, on a thing that fits in the palm of your hand.

But technology is not limited to just personal or business issues. The medical field has seen incredible advances thanks to technology. Most surgeries have become much easier to perform thanks to top of the line medical equipment. Blood tests have made it easier to diagnose and treat diseases. Research is going stronger than ever, and much, much more.

However, as I mentioned earlier, there are also downsides to technology. For instance, children have become glued to their tablets, phones and laptops and rarely, if ever, play outside anymore.

Our data is easier to steal than ever before, with some people finding their precious information sold on the dark web or finding out that their psychological profile has been documented even by “trustworthy” companies such as Facebook.

Even with these things in mind, I still think technology is beneficial to our society. The advantages far outweigh the disadvantages, in my honest opinion.
