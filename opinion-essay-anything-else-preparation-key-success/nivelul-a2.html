Nivelul A2

Written Subject 2 for level A2 (L3- adecvat tuturor filierelor, profilelor și specializărilor/ calificărilor):

Success is something that we all wish for, whether we choose to become a celebrity, a doctor, or a teacher.  Alexander Graham Bell once said that “Before anything else, preparation is the key to success”, although most people tend to get distracted by other things. 

Preparation is a good way to face problems, whether we are at school, at work, or at home. 

Firstly, preparation for a test or exam, for example, is not a priority for many students. Sometimes. we are distracted by things like Facebook or smartphones. 


Secondly, as students, we must do our homework, research and review in order to be able to pass an exam successfully because only being in class does not mean that we will get a good grade. 


However, I think that hard work and dedication are also very important.  Preparation helps us get an idea of the subject we are studying for, and it can also help us find different ways to approach a topic. 

In my opinion, in order to be successful one needs to not only be prepared, but also dedicated and hard working.
