Examenul de bacalaureat naţional 2014

Proba C
 de evaluare a competenţelor lingvistice într-o limbă de circulaţie internaţională studiată pe parcursul învăţământului liceal 

Proba de înţelegere a unui text audiat la Limba engleză

Toate filierele, profilurile şi specializările/ calificările

Subiectul I

You will hear a woman asking a man questions for a survey. For questions 1 – 4 choose the best answer (A, B, C or D). 

  1. Allen’s surname is 
    1. Seelvor.
    2. Sylver.
    3. Silver.
    4. Sallvor.
  2. His phone number is 
    1. 06171061.
    2. 16177161. 
    3. 06177063. 
    4. 26166064. 
  3. Allen’s nationality is
    1. British.
    2. American.
    3. French.
    4. Canadian.
  4. Allen works at
    1. a university.
    2. a secondary school.
    3. a high school. 
    4. a primary school.

Răspuns:

1. B
2. C
3. D
4. A

Subiectul al II-lea 

You will listen to a radio report on an archaeological discovery. For questions 5 – 10, choose the best answer (A, B, C or D). 

  1. The human remains the archaeologists found 
    1. could be those of King Richard III of England.
    2. could not be those of King Richard III of England.
    3. are certainly those of King Richard III of England.
    4. are not those of King Richard III of England.
  2. Richard became king 
    1. in 1452. 
    2. in 1542. 
    3. in 1483. 
    4. in 1458. 
  3. Richard’s reign was preceded by that of 
    1. Eduard
    2. Henry Tudor.
    3. Eduard IV. 
    4. King Henry VI. 
  4. King Richard III of England was 
    1. the first English king to die in a battle. 
    2. the only English king to die in a battle.
    3. the oldest English king to die in a battle.
    4. the last English king to die in a battle.
  5. Richard III was defeated by Henry Tudor,
    1. who ruled England for the next 20 years.
    2. who became King Henry Tudor.
    3. whose children and grandchildren also ruled England.
    4. who chose Leicester as the site of battle.
  6. Greyfriars Church, where Richard III was buried,
    1. still stands.
    2. has always been remembered by people.
    3. was replaced in the end by a car park.
    4. was rebuilt.

Răspuns:

5. A
6. C
7. C
8. D
9. C
10. C
