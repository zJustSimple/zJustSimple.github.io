Essay about change

Essay about change este o lucrare care privește unul dintre aspectele fundamentale ale vieții, schimbarea. Fie că este vorba de natură, viață, stare financiară sau socială, acest material ne arată că schimbarea este nu numai o parte esentială a experienței umane, este inevitabilă. Deși schimbarea sperie multă lume, eseul va încerca să îți schimbe nota, în bine.

Essay

“Everyone thinks of changing the world, but no one thinks of changing himself.”

Change is something that constantly occurs in nature, society, human personality, behavior and thought, etc. We see it everywhere, every day. The Sun’s cycle, the weather, the growing trees and plants, certain events that make history, kids turning into teenagers and then into adults are all part of the natural development known simply as “change”.

This phenomenon isn’t something that occurs instantly. It’s a gradual and constant process. Older cells die, while new cells are born, the planets rotate along the sun constantly, yet slowly when compared to the scale of the entire universe, we grow older gradually, and these changes aren’t so noticeable with the naked eye. These are things that we observe only after a while has passed. But as with any rule, there are also exceptions.

For instance, once a tragedy occurs, its impact is instant upon those affected. That can change people’s perspectives on things, their daily life, or even their mental and physical health. When someone dear to us passes away, we are drowned in sadness, we can barely concentrate on other things, our life and mind are affected instantly. On the brighter side, good change can also come as rapidly. When a new member of the family is born, your entire life shifts. You start loving someone new, you start caring for them, teaching them about everything and dedicate more of your time for them. Both these types of changes are instant but have lasting effects on us.

The most intelligent characteristic humans have over other species is the ability to adapt. We adapt to change better than any other life form. We built buildings to keep us warm in the winter. We built beds so we could sleep comfortably. We progressively changed the environment to fit our needs. Each change took place over a longer period of time. As the old saying goes “Rome wasn’t built in a day”. But we did it. No matter how long it took, we adapted. Adapting to change is crucial for survival. With all these advancements came technology. Technology is changing rapidly to fit all of our needs and wishes. 

With technological change, businesses have changed. It’s no longer just two people trading goods, but a huge global network for exchanging ideas, items and other values. We can now complete a transaction from the other side of the globe in mere seconds, whereas centuries ago we would have to travel by sea to make such arrangements. We develop things that can change people’s lives, such as medication, body enhancements for the crippled, pacemakers that can monitor a person’s heartbeat, and so on.

Change is something that we cannot avoid. Be it good or bad, it is inevitable and we have to muster all of our power to adapt it in order to survive. Sometimes, even one person can change the course of history. Always dream, be creative and think of ways to make this world a better place for all of us.

Essay about development

Dacă trebuie să faci un eseu despre dezvoltare, Essay about development îți vine în ajutor. Abordând subiectul dezvoltării din punct de vedere personal, istoric și industrial, Eseul ne arată rolul acestui fenomen, precum și condițiile necesare pentru ca dezvoltarea să decurgă conform asteptărilor. Fie că ai un test, un examen sau o temă de casă, Essay about development este o lucrare care îți va fi un aliat folositor în lupta pentru nota 10.

Essay

Development can be a long and hard process, no matter what it’s about. Whether we’re talking about human or technological development, there are several steps along our path to developing certain traits, items and whatnot. When speaking about human development we can think about three essential things: living a long and healthy life, acquiring knowledge and having a decent standard of living.

Of course, these traits are only essential, but not limited. Humans also need freedoms, rights, cooperation, education and others things in order to fully develop and become successful. When a human doesn’t have the right to speak freely and exchange ideas, their mind stagnates and subconsciously forces itself to accept pre-existing notions about society and other subjects, and thus we cannot say that their mind has developed. It’s actually pretty creepy to think about situations such as these.

On the other hand, if a human has all the necessities they need and profits off them in order to become skilled and, overall, a better person, we can say that they have developed. If they are helping others and building their path to success at the same time, we can assume that they have become better than a large portion of humans in our society. That is how I’d describe human development: becoming successful while not forgetting to help others. Why? Because we will only achieve a better society by working together.

Leaving aside the human part of development, let’s talk about technology and business. These situations are a little different because they require human input, unlike actual persons who can think on their own and thus develop independently without necessarily requiring assistance at all times. When talking about technology and business in the same sentence, most people think of companies such as Google, Microsoft and Apple. Many people might not know that these giants started out as small garage businesses. It wasn’t sheer luck that brought them to where they are today.

It was, however, innovation that brought them here. Working hours upon hours to perfect the written code, searching for ways to improve it, knowing how to market their company – these are all important aspects that these giants took seriously in order to evolve. Words are not enough to describe how long and complicated this process is, yet we have seen their small garages transform into huge headquarters that have affiliates all around the globe.

So, at the end of the day, what is development? To put it as simple as one can, it is the process of evolution. This process occurs naturally, but requires hard work and several outside factors depending on the context, as this course gets increasingly complicated with time. You can choose to develop your own self or your dreams, or even both at the same time. What matters is to stay focused and keep your heads up for upcoming challenges.

Essay about decision making

Vită sau pui, aici sau la pachet, roșu sau verde. Piața liberă și viața din secolul XXI ne oferă alegeri la fiecare pas, iar Essay about decision making este aici sa pună în perspectivă problema. Fie că este vorba de o simplă de masă de pranz sau despre facultatea la care vrei să mergi, Essay about decision making ne arată cum deciziile sunt o parte inevitabilă din viața noastră, și mai ales, cum putem sa îmbunătățim procesul decizional și odata cu el, consecințele. Ia decizia să citești Essay about decision making, pentru a beneficia de consecința unei note mari.

Essay

We make decisions on a daily basis, whether we realise it or not. Some decisions are extremely easy to make, such as what type of food you want to buy, what clothes you want to wear or at what time you want to go out with your friends. But occasionally we have to deal with decisions that are harder to make or that have certain moral implications attached to them, making it even harder to choose a path, as in some cases it’s not always like picking between black and white.

While growing up, you may have to decide what university you want to attend, what job you want to have, what company you want to work for and all sort of other similar matters. It’s a good thing that the free market is so wide and varied because you have a lot of choices to pick. But picking is not always easy, as there are many teenagers that have no idea what they want to do next or even what talent they have or what intrigues them and pique their curiosity. This problem is partly due to an underdeveloped educational system, but that is another topic altogether.

During adolescence you will encounter yet another “problem” that may present you with several decisions. Falling in love. More often than not, you will have to decide whether to interact with your crush in this very moment or wait for another opportunity, or you might have to decide what things to say and do in order to impress the person you love. No, I can’t give you an advice, because it depends on the situation at hand and on the person you’re trying to seduce, but if you’re in the position of loving two people at once, here’s what Johnny Depp had to say: “If you love two people at the same time, choose the second one. Because if you really loved the first one, you wouldn’t have fallen for the second.”

You may also encounter situations in your life where you have to choose between being honest and lying. Usually you tell the truth, especially if you love or respect an individual, but there are some acceptable exceptions. I lied to my grandfather when he was diagnosed with cancer, not because I didn’t love him or respect him, but because I didn’t want him to suffer more. His physical pain was already out of control, so the news would have been even more heart breaking. I lied to protect him, to give him hope. I don’t even want to imagine how he would’ve reacted if I told him the truth.

It goes without saying that political leaders and business owners have to make hard decisions almost every day. While we may not appreciate all of the choices these types of people make, we have to understand that morality and ethics differ greatly in a certain situation. Let’s just hope that the right people will be making the right choices that will ultimately impact the world in a good way. As a conclusion, I think making decisions should be a well thought process. Don’t jump the shark, instead think of all the possibilities before you act.

Essay about finding a job

Essay about finding a job este un eseu care adresează din mai multe perspective veșnica problemă a găsirii unui loc de muncă. Deși pare o activitate trivială, și vedem aproape zilnic anunțuri de angajare în jurul nostru, Essay about finding a job ne explică diferitele probleme cu acest proces aparent simplu. De la obținerea experienței, la salar și la satisfacția sufletească a unui loc de muncă, Essay about finding a job încearcă să acopere cât mai multe aspecte ale acestei teme.

Essay

Taking into consideration that high school is almost over, we must think about either going to university, finding a job or do both at the same time. I’m personally thinking about taking a gap year between finishing high school and enrolling into a university, partly because I want to see how it is to manage on my own. Although there aren’t many work options available for high school graduates, I think that this experience ultimately helps you better understand life itself and how our society and economic system functions.

My parents always worked hard in order to ensure that I get whatever I want and that they will always satisfy my needs. Having a job means that I will be able to take care of myself from now on and that I may eventually repay my mom and dad for all the support that they have offered me throughout my life. Seeing their child take care of himself will also make them proud, which will inevitably make me happy. We may have had a few arguments throughout the years, but I will never forget the fact that they stood by my side through good and bad, and there’s nothing that would make me happier than making them proud and offering back the support that they gave me throughout the years.

What other reasons do I have to get a job so early in my life? As stated earlier, getting a job offers us a view of the real world, beyond our adolescent dreams. By seeking a job, you inevitably seek self-improvement, because having a job equals having responsibilities and improving your skills. Working within a company means that you will learn a lot about ethics, the importance of communication and the importance of meeting deadlines and being rewarded for everything that you did right. I know this because everyone who’s older than me seem to agree on this aspect. One friend of mine even said that life truly begins with your first job.

Some people might try getting a part time job while also attending university. This is a great way to learn how to manage your time and gather the resources necessary to have a good student life, but it can also hinder your learning. I think that learning how to balance a schedule is the key to organizing all of our tasks, but it can also be a hassle if you’ve got a lot of things to do in a short period of time. In any case, it’s still worth a try, even if it is just to see how well you can adjust.

Those are my thoughts about finding a job. I know that many people might not share my opinion and I can understand that. Whatever you might think, at one point in your life, you’re still going to need a job, and I think that getting one sooner rather than later represents an advantage over others, especially in the highly competitive market sectors.

Essay about future career

Dacă te-ai chinuit vreodată să-ți găsești un job, Essay about future career îți poate fi de folos. Fiind un pas la care ajungem cu toții într-un punct al vieții noastre, Essay about future career ne enumeră câteva dintre opțiunile care ne pot ajuta în acest pas important. De la consiliere, până la freelancing Essay about future career ne explică cum sa ne folosim din plin de fiecare dintre aceste opțiuni arătându-ne avantajele și dezavantajele.

Essay

At some stage in your life it’s inevitable to think about the future of your career. Many people have trouble finding something that they like doing, that also earns them enough money. Don’t be worried if you are one of those people, because we have all been there at some point in our life. Instead of worrying, try exploring different options and see what best fits you.

One easy way to seek help regarding your career option is to take quizzes online. But not one of those basic quizzes. I’m talking about the complicated ones, the ones that actually take time to complete. Those are pretty good at asking the right questions and even making you question yourself and your own abilities and likes. Suffice to say that once you start completing this sort of task you will be more open to exploring different options.

Another option that takes more time, yet yields more efficient results is to seek career counselling. If you are a student, chances are high that there’s a department specialized in this domain. Be sure to check this out, because it will help you in the long run. I had this opportunity presented to me, took advantage of it and I can say that it has helped me tremendously. Not only did I discuss ideas about the types of jobs I should seek, but I also learned how to write an impressive CV, cover letter and letter of intent. Always pay attention when writing these things, as they give out the first impression to those who might hire you, and the first impression is the most important.

A third way to finding a path in your future career is to search for job fairs in your area. These fairs usually offer a wide variety of career and internship offers. Some might be scared of the fact that internships usually go unpaid, but do not worry, because they still grant you the extra experience you may need in order to find a better paying job. We all have to start somewhere on our path and the road is not always easy, it’s just a matter of hard work and never letting opportunities slide between your fingertips.

Yet one other great way to build your career is freelancing. Although this doesn’t guarantee a stable income, depending on the type of contract you’re on, it does, however, offer the opportunity of a slow but steady start for your career and it represents yet one more attribute that you can write down as experience on your CV. There are lots of websites that cater to both freelancers who are searching for work and companies or people who want to hire said freelancers.

There are plenty of domains to choose from and chances are that finding the one which suites you are not too far away. Don’t lose your hope even if it seems like you’re stuck. Everyone fails from time to time, there is no shame in that. The important thing is to rise back up after you’ve fallen and never give up, no matter how hard the situation might seem.

Essay about newspapers

Essay about newspapers este o lucrare care aduce înapoi în centrul atenției, chiar dacă doar pentru câteva minute, bătrânul nostru prieten, ziarul. Deși este o formă de mediatizare uitată sau pur și simplu învechită pentru majoritatea oamenilor, Essay about newspapers amintește câteva dintre meritele titlurilor mari de pe hârtia subțire. Eseul este o lucrare informativă care face apel la elemente istorice, și ne arată că deși nu are ecran LED, ziarul își merită locul ca și unul dintre marile progrese tehnologice ale ultimului mileniu.

Essay

Newspapers represent a nearly dead medium through which to transmit news. They have been defeated by other forms of media that do the exact same thing: social networks, the internet as a whole, television and radio, but their immense contribution to informing the public can not go unnoticed.

Like all media, newspapers also contained ads, alongside all the news, to ensure that the company who wrote and printed them could make a larger profit in order to sustain production. Thus, this became the sure way to get stories out to the vast majority of the public. Keep in mind that television was yet to be invented and the internet seemed like a concept ripped straight from the science fiction category. Before printing presses, the only way to get news out was to either announce it in public through people who would constantly shout out headlines, or by writing on posters and sticking them all over town. Alas, this method only worked if you wanted to inform a small portion of the population, so something new was needed.

Thus came the newspaper, manufactured by the printing machines of the time. They allowed for more versatility when it came to organizing the words, images and their placement and sizes. They made mass production as easy as pushing a button, because they no longer needed to be written or drawn on by hand, instead being copied over and over again to meet the necessary market demand. Although a technological wonder for that time, they were not far away from controversy.

News outlets often had to fight against lobbyists and lawmakers to ensure that they had the freedom to print whatever they wished. It was part of the movement for freedom. In the US, the first amendment from the constitution guarantees the freedom of press, which now practically translates to freedom for any news outlet, but back then, the printing machine set the standard for this valuable liberty. 

Had it not been for that right, the internet wouldn’t be as we know it today. News articles would be heavily censored, opinion articles would be almost nonexistent and freedom of speech might still be on the discussion table in the government even now. Although primitive by today’s standards, newspapers have enabled us to get to this point, to have the freedom to be informed, they have made idea sharing as flexible as ever.

Of course, while the newspaper itself may die, its legacy will still stand. We now have online newspapers, although it would be kind of wrong to call them that, as they do not necessitate paper. But the brands that began the news printing business still stand strong today, in digital form. The need for paper might not be as high, but the need for information will always be enormous.
