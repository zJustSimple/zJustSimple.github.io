Essay about nature

Essay about nature este un eseu descriptiv redactat de echipa Liceunet care poate fi folosit cu încredere pentru a rezolva cerința ”Write a descriptive essay about nature„. Eseul conține descrieri detaliate scrise într-un limbaj familiar, structurate si verificate pentru a mulțumi si cei mai exigenți profesori. Essay about nature este de folos atat in cazul cerintei specifice, cat si pentru exemplificarea unei structuri generale a eseului descriptiv.

Essay

Nature is everything which surrounds us, that was not built by man. All the beautiful, fresh flowers, all the blooming trees, the beautiful blue sky, the shining sun, the puffy clouds and all the wonderful animals. Nature is our friend that offers us everything we need to live on this planet, everything that we can admire and makes us happy.

Nothing feels quite like taking a walk through a dense forest on a Saturday. Walking on all the unpaved paths, listening to the birds singing, taking in the fresh air which gives you energy and fills you with joy, staying at a camp fire late in the night and then laying on the ground while admiring the stars above. Nothing can beat that feeling.

The best part in hiking through the forest is when you do it with your friends. Simply exploring the woods is wonderful, yet staying the night in a cabin or in a tent after extinguishing the camp fire can soothe a person’s nerves better than many other activities. And, as they say, “sharing is caring”, so why not share these amazing experiences with the people you love? Amazing people, plus a day or a night outdoors is a sure way both to have fun and to relax. Your batteries will surely get fully charged once you’ve done this over the weekend, so you’ll be at your best on Monday when school starts.

But what else can you do when it comes to nature? Well, there’s also mountain climbing, which can be tiring but very entertaining. You might encounter difficult paths in such a sport, but that’s the catch: to train your abilities in nature exploration and make it to the top, both literally and metaphorically. Even when writing this, I can feel my heart pumping, I can feel sweat crawling down my back, I can feel my lungs burning, but I can also feel the immense satisfaction when I reach that 3000 meters altitude goal.

At that height, the air is as fresh as it can get. Absolutely no pollution, no audio interference from the outside world, it’s just you and nature. It’s just you and the world at your fingertips. Look down below and you shall see the vast valley full of wondrous trees covered by mist. It’s simply breathtaking and gorgeous.

If you’re not a very sporty person, but would like to take in nature at its fullest, you could try driving on a curvy mountain road. This is a pretty fun driving experience, although caution is advised, and the landscape definitely adds a lot to it. Unfortunately, not all forests or mountains have paved roads, so you’re out of luck in certain locations. But, where you do have the possibility, be sure to go ahead and drive with care.

Overall, nature is beautiful, it is worth going outdoors through the forests and mountains at least a few times a year, even just to escape the crowded city life. You’d be doing yourself a huge favor by going to places such as these, because you will be happy at the end of the day, even after a tiresome road.

Essay about the relationship between humans and nature

Essay about the relationship between humans and nature este o lucrare care pune în discuție o problemă interesantă, relația dintre oameni și natură. Trăind în secolul tehnologiei, într-o junglă de beton inconjurați de mașini și semafoare, Essay about the relationship between humans and nature ne amintește de relațiile dintre oameni și mediul care ne înconjoară, punând accent pe atitudinea naturii față de noi și invers.

Essay

Humans have always been related to nature in several ways. It is true that nature isn’t always kind with us, but it is unintentional, because nature does not think, nature is just taking its natural course. Humans have also treated nature unpleasantly from time to time, with the difference being that humans have sometimes done this intentionally.

Humans constantly pollute the environment that gives us all the basic necessities to live. Trees take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen, which humans desperately need, yet we cut down trees and turn them into furniture and paper. Nature helps us create so many things that represent an advantage for us without asking for anything in return. We take what we can from nature without offering back something.

Unfortunately, our acts of polluting nature have caused great harm that could hurt us all one day. Global warming is increasing at an alarming rate each year due to pollution and similar factors. This will not only affect nature, but us humans as well, and it is our own doing. Driving in cars, building factories and throwing garbage everywhere have slowly but steadily contributed to the rise of the average global temperature.

These things have caused a greenhouse effect which subsequently made the Earth’s atmosphere thicker, thus trapping more heat from the Sun. Our interference with nature has ultimately affected its course. We are starting to see the harm we have done to our planet in the last century, from wars to pollution, to oil leaks and so on. Instead of consuming all of nature’s nourishment, without doing anything in return for it, we should all be part of cleaning the environment. 

Moreover, if we help nature grow, we will be repaid substantially. More trees mean more oxygen and less greenhouse gasses. Renouncing the use of cars for short distances can make the air in the city feel just a little cleaner. Throwing trash in appropriate bins instead of throwing it on the ground helps the world look better. Small steps must be taken to ensure that our relationship with nature goes back to normal. We have evolved so much now that we can make technology from renewable sources, so why waste what is left of the planet?

Our relationship with nature isn’t too steady as of yet, but we still have a lot of time to fix things. No matter how small your contribution to the struggle may be, if we all unite and fight this together, we will surely win.

Essay about nature's beauty

Essay about nature's beauty este o lucrare care ne aduce aminte de originile vieții, mama natură. Fiind înconjurați în fiecare zi din ce în ce mai mult de jungla de beton, Essay about nature's beauty încearcă să deschidă fereastra metaforică pentru a ne arăta frumusețea mediului dincolo de ciment și semafoare. Essay about nature's beauty amintește meritele naturii în viața noastră, puterea și imporțanta grijii care trebuie să i-o purtăm.

Essay

Nature is always full of wonder and things that need to be seen in real life in order to truly appreciate their beauty. We tend to ignore going for a stroll through the woods far too often, as the majority of people tend to migrate to urban areas. I’m not implying that cities aren’t beautiful, in fact I think it’s marvelous that humans have created so many astounding beauties, but I do consider that admiring the simple things in life is just as important, if not even crucial in order to get a sense of our environment.

I think it’s astounding how nature seems to die and then be reborn every year. The four seasons could be considered a metaphor about human life. Spring is the beginning, as flowers and trees flourish in their colourful beauty. Summer is the young adult, filled with energy and joy, while autumn represents an old, dying entity. Winter itself could be compared to death, as trees have already lost all of their leaves and their branches lie naked. But unlike humans, nature continues its cycle effortlessly, for it is during spring time that it is reborn.

When I think of nature’s beauty, I think of lush forests, immense mountains, beautiful waterfalls, shining sunlight, pale moonlight, the numerous stars above us, all the different flowers and trees and all the nourishment the land has to offer. Simply put, nature is astounding. I couldn’t imagine any person not being impressed by all its glorious beauty. Come to think about it, even the things that we humans built are made from things found in nature. Yes, even chemicals come from it. When you look at Mendeleev’s table of elements, you can see just how complex life is.

Even when you’re driving a car, you inevitably interact with nature, because the fuel in your vehicle is what remains of the vast jungles of plants and trees that covered this planet millions of years ago. Simply thinking about it this way is incredible. Even after so much time has passed, these creatures, who were created by nature, still roam around us in a whole new way. Even in death, nature still flourishes and finds new ways to serve us. That fact alone is simply mind blowing and makes you wonder what other possibilities we have not yet discovered.

Think about the fact that this planet survived an ice age, clashes with meteorites, volcano eruptions, nuclear catastrophes such as Chernobyl or Fukushima and atomic bombs like the ones thrown over Hiroshima and Nagasaki during World War 2. Nature truly is glorious and resilient. We keep polluting the planet, yet it still firmly survives. We keep waging wars over all sorts of issues, yet nature still stands still, patiently waiting to flourish once again.

Overall, no one can doubt how amazing this planet is and how lucky we are to inhabit it. Make no mistake, just because nature is strong and resilient does not mean that we should find more ways to tamper with it. We have to make good use of nature, we have to work along with it, not against it, because nature is a friend that never leaves our side.
