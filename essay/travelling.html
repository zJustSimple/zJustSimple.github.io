Essay about travelling

Dacă vreodată ai fost întrebat cum este să călătorești, dar nu ți-ai găsit cuvintele, Essay about travelling te va putea ajuta. Existând atâtea metode de a călători și locuri numai bune de vizitat, Essay about travelling descrie câteva dintre metodele în care poți vedea priveliști ce pot fi admirate doar în afara limitelor propriului oraș. Essay about travelling pune la dispoziția cititorului o lucrare de nota 10 care încurajează dezvoltarea personală prin călătorie, și cultivă spiritul de aventură.

Essay

“The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page.”

Travelling should be any person’s hobby when they have enough free time to indulge in this sort of activity. Fortunately, the Earth is very large and there are all sorts of countries with diverse culture that you may want to explore. There are a lot of landscapes and different landforms that you can admire. Most times, even your own country may surprise you with such beauty that you may want to explore before even planning on going somewhere else.

Some may object with the argument that traveling isn’t cheap, though technically speaking the act itself is free. It’s the commodities that cost. If you’re the adventurer type, you could always try travelling by foot, though you will need to take large amounts of supplies with you on your journey.

Fortunately, if you have the necessary money, you don’t have to go through all the hassle of getting to a destination by walking. One preferred, though expensive method of travelling between countries is by airplane. But, as many can assure you, the cost is worth, as there is no other feeling in the world much like it. Seeing all the mountains, seas and cities from such a great height is a wonder in its own right. Few words exist that can describe the way a beautifully lit city looks during the night time when you’re in an airplane, or how glorious the mountains can be when viewed from a bird’s eye perspective.

But, alas, there are cheaper ways of travelling for those who can’t afford this luxury. For students, the preferred method is to travel by train. A train compartment is roomy enough to accommodate a group of friends who are travelling together, so you don’t have to worry about being seated further away from one another like on an airplane. The downside to this is that it’s a slow method, especially depending on the type of train, the stability and durability of the tracks and, of course, the weather conditions.

If you have a car, then you’ve got yet another method for traveling. A car can usually fit 5 people inside and you can plan your journey ahead of time, so will know which roads you can take, where you can stop to eat and drink or where you can park to admire some incredible views. There are, however, disadvantages with this method also. Unless you have at least one more friend with a driver’s license, it can be very tiresome to drive long distances. You have to be careful not to run out of fuel, you have to find parking spaces, and that itself is hard in heavily populated urban areas.

Overall, you should pick and choose your preferred traveling method, gather up some money and be on your way, because the world is vast and full of wonders and it would be a shame not to explore it. If you do so, you will have the time of your life and inevitably remain with some great stories which you can tell to your friends and family.

Essay about the summer holiday

Essay about the summer holiday este o lucrare care descrie perioada noastră preferată din an, vacanța de vară. Chiar dacă Bacalaureatul bate la ușă, și încearcă să intre pe geam, Essay about the summer holiday ne amintește de experiențele frumoase din timpul verii, și de posibilitățile pe care ni le oferă acest anotimp primitor. Descarcă Essay about the summer holiday pentru a-ți găsi inspirația, atât pentru următorul test, cât și pentru planurile de dupa Bac.

Essay

Summer is the perfect season to relax, travel and have fun. The summer holiday lasts 3 months, which is perfect to do all of these things. The sun is shining, the weather is good, tourism flourishes, people are eager to go places, meet new acquaintances and have tons of fun at the beach, at the club or anywhere they can.

During the summer, I like exploring the world as much as I can. If possible, I would like to go on vacation to another country and visit as many sites as I can. I like admiring beautiful landscapes and hiking through the forest. I like going to the beach and sit back as the sun hits my skin, or go into the water and swim as much as humanly possible. I enjoy traveling and meeting new people a lot. That’s why I usually take my vacation to a big city when possible, because I know that a lot of tourists are also interested in having fun and meeting new friends.

Some of these things may seem contradictory to one another – hiking through the woods and going to a big city, but they do not necessarily exclude each other. Anyone needs both some time alone and some time with other people. Although fun, socializing can be tiresome at times and having some precious time for yourself is recommended. As with anything, it is important to have a certain balance in your life. I like keeping this balance, that’s why I seek doing all of these things during the summer. If that is not possible, then I alternate between socializing and relaxing from one vacation to another.

Of course, there are also trips along with family members. When I was a child, I usually went to the seaside with my parents. Even though I was too young to remember too much, I can clearly recall that I enjoyed those times. My parents love me and take good care of me, they always worked hard to make me happy, and this was no exception during the summer holiday. I am very thankful that I have them and I consider myself lucky. Although we as teenagers often ignore our parents because we feel ashamed of being seen with older relatives, we still need to find time to spend with them.

There are so many things I am looking forward to this summer that it is hard to pick just a few activities. I have never been skydiving, scuba diving or jet skiing ever and those things sound like a lot of fun. I might just consider doing them if I get the opportunity. Or maybe I should go surfing or bungee jumping, but I’ll admit it, I’m kind of scared. There are so many options to take into consideration that even 3 months seems like very little time to be able to accomplish them all. Nevertheless, we should all strive to do as much fun and relaxing activities as we can during the summer, because it is the perfect time to try everything and go anywhere you want.

Essay about holidays

Fie că vorbim despre Crăciun, Revelion sau sărbătorile Pascale, ”Essay about holidays” ne poate fi de folos. Dacă ai fost vreodată în vacanță, Essay about holidays te poate ajuta să transpui acele experiențe pe foaie. Amintind sărbătorile și vacanțele pe care le știm și le iubim cu toții, lucrarea Essay about holidays prezintă în mare evenimentele care ne dau liber de la muncă și de la școală, aducând în discuție și diferite moduri de a petrece acest timp liber bine meritat.

Essay 

The holidays are a special time for everyone who is a student or an employee. It is a time that people spend with their families or go on a trip to some special place. During this period, it is essential to relax and take a break from mundane life, especially given the fact that usually time is too limited for anything out of the ordinary and the weekends are short.

There are, of course, several different holidays throughout the year. Some of them are longer and some are a little shorter, but depending on the month and the weather, you can try all sorts of things or go to several different places in that period.

For example, during the winter holidays, especially on Christmas Eve, most people spend their time with their family at home, decorating the tree, listening to carols and whatnot. It is a beautiful feeling, reminiscent of childhood memories, when you were waiting for Santa Claus to come and give you presents. A very soothing feeling, indeed, but just a week after this day, people will celebrate New Year’s Eve, and that is a whole different story from Christmas.

People usually like going to parties that night, But the big question for everyone is where exactly will they spend that evening. Some prefer going to the mountains, in a cabin, skiing until the sun sets and then watching firework comfortably on the balcony. Others prefer going to a pub or a restaurant in town and party until morning. There are tons of options for a night like this, some more expensive than others, but with good friends you are bound to find something that suits you.

Yet another major holiday occurs around the time of Easter. During this time frame you can enjoy all the wonders of spring, so choosing a destination should be quite easy and varied. You could easily go on a picnic in the woods with friends and family, take a trip to one of the major cities in your countries, as they have a fairly large Easter Market during this time, or if you’re a religious person, there are several church ceremonies held in the days near Easter.

But who could forget the big one? The summer holiday is the longest one for students. This season offers many opportunities for travelling, relaxation and fun. You might enjoy going to the beach, seaside, or go to the mountains for some quiet time with nature in all its splendor, or you could also go to a city that you always wanted to visit, or maybe take a bite out of several music festivals. The choices in this time frame are very numerous, so you should have no problem picking something that fits your needs.

Let’s not forget other small holidays that may apply to certain countries. Those vary from place to place so you will have to do a bit of research about what you could do in those respective periods depending where you live or where you want to go.

In conclusion, holidays are fun and enjoyable. Depending on which one we are talking about, there are all sorts of thing to do in order to relax yourself and recharge your batteries for the restart of school or your job. So take advantage of days like these and do whatever pleases you.

Essay about adventure

Dacă îți place să escaladezi munți, să vizitezi orașe noi sau să mergi la festivale, Essay about adventure este lucrarea pentru tine. Punând toate aceste activități în categoria aventurii, Essay about adventure ne explică cum acest concept include o multitudine de lucruri, de la o simplă plimbare până la călatorii îndrăznețe în locuri nemaivăzute. Oricare ar fi idealul tău de aventură, lucrarea te poate ajuta să-l transpui pe foaie, aducându-ți un 10 la următorul test și poate chiar să te ajute să îl transformi în realitate.

Essay

What is adventure? It is an unforgettable experience, it means doing outstanding things and creating memories. You may say that life itself is an adventure. Throughout our entire existence, we do magnificent things that make up a lot of our most cherished memories. Adventures can be something as mundane as going out on a Saturday with friends or as unique and captivating as bungee jumping.

By classic definition, adventure is defined as a risky undertaking with an unknown outcome, but in reality, it varies from person to person. One might say that an adventure can also be something that you do for the first time, something that makes you feel truly alive, an experience that you would describe as the most fun you’ve ever had. My opinion is that adventure isn’t something set in stone, it’s something that we make for ourselves, it’s something that relies solely on our own preferences and feelings.

For me, the entire summer holiday could be considered an adventure. I like going to music festivals and meeting new people. I like going to the seaside and swim all day long. I like climbing mountains and exploring vast forests. These are a few of the things I do during summer time and, for me, these are adventures. These are experiences that make me feel as if I’m living life to its fullest potential. Going to festivals and meeting new people is like a new experience every time, you never know what the outcome is going to be. Swimming in the sea and climbing mountains makes for tremendous exercise. It makes you feel that you are one with the Earth, and nothing is more adventurous than conquering nature.

Another thing that I consider a true adventure, but I never got to do, is going to the jungle and exploring the wilderness, much like the people at Discovery Channel or National Geographic do. Yes, I know that they are trained professionals who are well equipped for survival in such situations, but it would be lots of fun to undertake such tasks. It’s one thing to view nature on a TV or computer screen and a whole other experience to view it and explore it in real life. Sure, you could say that if I wanted to see wildlife I could go to the zoo, but it’s nowhere near as exciting as seeing wild animals free, doing their own thing without being restricted by cages.

In the end, I think that the classic definition of adventure doesn’t matter. What matters is that the thing you’re experiencing feels adventurous to you. Don’t stay locked indoors in front of your computer all day, go to new places, travel as much as you can, meet as many people as you can, do new things, get out of your comfort zone. You have nothing to lose, but the world to gain. Always seek adventure, don’t just stand there waiting for a miracle. If you want something, go and take it.
