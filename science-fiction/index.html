Introduction

On this page, we will provide an overview of some of the most famous English-language works of science fiction literature from the past couple of centuries, as well as some examples of influential science fiction movies. 

Note that we have also indexed some short stories by Ray Bradbury on his author page.

Science fiction novels from the 19th century

Shelley, Mary - Frankenstein; or, the Modern Prometheus (1818)

Wells, H.G. - The Time Machine (1885)

Wells, H.G. - The War of the Worlds (1898)
