Characters

There are several different characters in the play. Among them is Hamlet, the main character, Claudius, Hamlet's uncle and also the villain of the play, Gertrud, Hamlet's mother, The Ghost who is the recently deceased father of Hamlet, Ophelia, the one whom Hamlet is in love with, Laertes, the brother of Ophelia, and Polonius, the father of Ophelia and Laertes.

Hamlet

Hamlet is the protagonist of the play. He is the prince of Denmark, and is ambitious, bitter and full of hatred
for his uncle. He knows what he is doing, even though it doesn't always seem like it. Hamlet wants to get
some proof that his uncle really killed the king. He Among other things pretends to be crazy. Hamlet also
gets some actors to fake the scene of how his father died. He also is a character that admires other characters
like Fortinbras and the Player King. Hamlet can sometimes be indecisive and hesitant, but at other times, he
can have an impulsive act of violence

Ophelia

Ophelia is the young beautiful daughter of Polonius. She is in love with Hamlet, but since she is really dutiful, she strives to obey her father, Polonius, who doesn't like her being so much around Hamlet. When her father dies, she looses her sanity, and while in her madness she paints a picture of men being unfaithful. She also has mad speeches about flowers: “Look at my flowers. There's rosemary, that's for remembering. Please remember, love. And there are pansies, they're for thoughts”. There aren't long afterwards that she commits suicide.

Claudius

Claudius is the villain of the play. He is also the uncle of Hamlet. He is calculating, and willing to execute, murder and assassinate to stay in power. He doesn't understand Hamlet's motives, but he is very quick to see him as a threat.

The Ghost

The Ghost is the deceased king and father of Hamlet. He shows up in the middle of the night and tells Hamlet what his own brother did to him, “Now listen, Hamlet. Everyone was told that a poisonous snake bit me when I was sleeping in the orchard. But in fact, that's a lie that's fooled everyone in Denmark. You should know, my noble son, the real snake that stung you father is now wearing his crown.” He wants Hamlet to avenge him, but Hamlet doesn't know for certain that the ghost is who it claims to be.

Other characters

Some other characters you will meet throughout the play is Horatio, Hamlet's close friend, Fortinbras, the young prince of Norway, Rosencrantz and Guildenstern, two of Hamlet's former friends from Wittenberg. You will also meet Marcellus and Barnado, the two watchmen.
