Nivelul B1

Written Subject 1 for level B1 (L2 - adecvat tuturor filierelior, profilurilor și specializărilor/calificărilor).

Observă cu atenție textul redactat de profesorii de engleză:

Dear John, 

How are things? 

I am writing you because I have decided to apply for a course at one of the colleges in London. I would like you to assist me in finding the most appropriate institution. 

Firstly, I have been studying economy for the past three years, and I would like to become an accountant. Also, I have taken a Cambridge B2 exam, and I have a good level of English. 

I really hope that you can help me in choosing the best course at the right college. 

Looking forward to hearing from you soon. 

Love, 

Linda.
