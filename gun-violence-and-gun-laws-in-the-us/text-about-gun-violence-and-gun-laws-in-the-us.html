Speeches

Barack Obama's Remarks on Reducing Gun Violence

Obama's speech took place in the aftermath of the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting. The main topic that US President Barack Obama focuses on in his remarks about reducing gun violence is the importance of gun control regulations being passed by Congress.

Barack Obama's Remarks on Common-Sense Gun Safety Reform

In his speech, Barack Obama emphasizes the urgency of limiting gun violence in the United States. He also intends to convince and pressure Congress – led by Republicans – to act upon new gun control measures.

Barack Obama's Statement on the Shooting in Oregon

The speech took place in 2015 as a response to the shooting in Oregon. Among other topics, Obama talks about the absurdity of asking for fewer gun-control laws and more guns in America, which is already affected by waves of mass-shootings
