Introduction

On this page, we will provide an overview of some of the most famous works of fantasy literature from the past couple of centuries, as well as some examples of influential fantasy movies. 

The works are listed alphabetically according to the author, producer, or director. Note that the literary works are mainly novels, which make up series. There are a few short stories, though. 

Fantasy literature up until the 19th century

Carroll, Lewis - Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland (1865)

Carroll, Lewis - Through the Looking-Glass, and What Alice Found There (1872)

MacDonald, George - Phantastes (1858)

Morris, William - The Well at the World’s End (1896)
