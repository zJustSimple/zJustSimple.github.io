Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

The story in the third novel of the series, Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, is about a thirteen years old boy called Harry Potter. He lives at his Uncle and aunts house, because his own parents were murdered by the dangerous Lord Voldemort.

On Harry's birthday his uncles sister visit them. Harry hates her. She always says mean things to him and this time was no excuse. His uncle had told her that he was going on a school for kids who made crime, because they were ashamed that he was going on a school for witches and wizards, and then she said that she wasn’t surprised est. And that was the last straw to break the camel's back. He blows her up like a balloon, and then he took all his stuff and ran as fast as he could down the road. When he was far away from the house, he stopped to get the breath. Suddenly he heard something and looked up and then he saw a purple bus coming near him and then it stopped right in front of him, and a little man came out. “Will you onboard at the night bus, we can take you everywhere?” the little man said. Harry asked him if it could take him to the leaky cauldron, and of cause it could. Harry went into the bus and before he knew of it, the bus drove as fast as a jumbo jet. When he came to the leaky cauldron he checked in. He was going to stay there for 2 weeks, until his best friends Ron and Hermione came, so they could take the Hogwarts Express to Hogwarts together. One of the days he found out that, one of the most dangerous prisoners had escaped from the prison in Azkaban, and that his name was Sirius Black. 

When the day came, where they should go to Hogwarts, they walked down to the station and went into the wall between 9 ¾ and out on the other side where the train was holding. They walked into the train, but there weren’t any empties cabins, so they went into one, where one of the new professors at Hogwarts was sleeping. When they had been driving in about 2 hours, the train suddenly stopped and it felt like someone went onboard, and so there did. 

After a little while a black see-through ghost came into the cabin and then Harry fainted. When he waked again the Professor was also awake. The professor handed him a piece of chocolate. “It will help you feel better”, the professor said. He told them that it was one of the Dementors who were looking after Sirius Black.

Later in the book, Harry found out that the reason why Sirius had escaped from the prison was because he wants to kill Harry and that Sirius had been best friends with his father, and that Sirius had betrayed them. He also found out that the professor from the train was helping Sirius and that Sirius was his godfather. 

My opinion is that I think it was a very good book. It was very exciting and it was also very scary. You can’t stoop reading it when you started to read because it was so good. I was so surprised about how easy it actually was to read in English. And it will not be the last time I read a Harry Potter in English again.
